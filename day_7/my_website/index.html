Me, Myself, and I

I am 20 years old and probably the youngest out of everyone in the class. I may have less life experience but please dont mistake this for complete ignorance. I work best in a group setting allowing everyone to bounce ideas off eachother. This way of working is great for creating the best solution for each problem. To obtain the best solution, I ask you to outright tell me when I am wrong.

I moved to Denver about 10 months ago and I am amazed by how many things there are to do here! I grew up in a small town of Iowa, so my interests were limited to working on the farm, highschool sports, and women. Since I have been living here I have been near the mountains at all times. I have been able to snowboard whenever I want, instead of doing it during the busiest times of the year. I have also been introduced to climbing although I am not very good, it is an absolute blast. Having access to the beautiful scenery is always a plus. Since graduated highschool last year, I have also taken up playing the guitar. It's always exciting to learn new chord progressions and songs. It is an endless hobby just like computer programmming!

Since moving here, I have also taken a great interest in philosphy, specifically Eastern. There is something about gaining access to the way everything around us works together and why, that gets my blood flowing. I am very embarrassed to say I cannot do drugs and alcohol like a normal person. I have decided to be sober because of how much I destroy my life on substances. Since deciding this, I have been able to gain control back I had thought was impossible. Turing is giving me the chance to make my life great and I am beyond excited to learn the language of devices we use everyday.

Module 0 has been a huge learning experience. I was really nervous going into this, thinking I wasnt going to understand anything. The amount of resources available were great in getting the hang of things.I focused on enhacing my productivity because I was lacking in that area by a long shot. Here are some of the resources I used to to do so and if you havent used them i'd give them a shot.
